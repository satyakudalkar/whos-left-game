Actions

In setup, each player gets one private turn to choose a secret. After that, only the current questioner can pass control. When you are answering, your full board is hidden and you only keep your private secret plus the opponent mini-board. A correct guess locks both boards until reset.

Your Board

The first two turns are for picking secrets. After setup, only the current questioner sees the full board. The answering player keeps only the secret panel and the opponent mini-board. Use Guess to make a direct call on the opponent's secret.
