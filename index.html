Who's Left?

A 2-player peer-to-peer companion board for Discord calls. You track your Bollywood grid, the other player sees only masked card positions flipping on their mini-board, and both sides stay synced over WebRTC with manual copy/paste signaling.

Not connected

Connection

Mode support is data-driven. The MVP ships with Bollywood only.

Manual Signaling

Create an offer or paste a remote offer to begin.

  1. Host clicks Create Offer and sends the generated text.
  2. Joiner pastes it, clicks Create Answer, then sends back the answer.
  3. Host pastes that answer and clicks Accept Answer.
